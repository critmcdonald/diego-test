Welcome to my website!

I am a multimedia student journalist at the University of Texas pursuing a journalism degree with a certificate in computer science. With a passion for writing and computational techniques, I specialize in data-driven storytelling, visualization and multimedia reporting. I am interested in exploring use cases of evolving technologies for journalistic purposes, as well as their impact on the field.

Learn more about me on my about page.
